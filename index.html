Stream 1:

Cost Efficiency

Spend less, safely
Benefit Business impact
  • Reduced governance and compliance costs without increasing risk
Value Props Experienced gain
  • Only pay for training staff actually need
  • Avoid blanket retraining while maintaining assurance
Mechanism How it works
  • Verify what staff already know before assigning training
  • Identify and target genuine knowledge gaps
Features What exists
  • KV assessments (assessment-first option)
  • Eligibility rules (who can verify vs must train)
  • Reassignment flow (train only when needed)
Stream 2:

Governance Control

Risk tolerance stays yours
Benefit Business impact
  • Defensible training decisions aligned to organisational risk tolerance
Value Props Experienced gain
  • Stay in control with policy-aligned thresholds
  • Make defensible training decisions aligned to risk tolerance
Mechanism How it works
  • Set pass marks, depth, and eligibility to match organisational risk
  • Generate defensible evidence of competence (not just completion)
Features What exists
  • Configurable pass marks and thresholds
  • Assessment depth controls (question bank size / selection)
  • LO / complexity tagging + audit-ready reporting
Stream 3:

Operational Efficiency

Less admin drag
Benefit Business impact
  • More staff capacity for high-impact work (with less admin overhead)
Value Props Experienced gain
  • Redirect staff time to higher-value activities
  • No added operational complexity to adopt KV
Mechanism How it works
  • Automate assignment, tracking, and evidence generation
  • Reduce manual chasing and reporting overhead
Features What exists
  • Automated assignment + re-assignment
  • Completion tracking + staff performance views
  • Reporting exports / payroll integration
